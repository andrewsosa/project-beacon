The DIY POI Finder!

Beacon is an Android application that helps you find points of interest. Points like vending machines, bathrooms, or scenic spots.


Lightweight and Easy to use!

We tied to make an intuitive and streamlined method for our users to keep track of, look up, and add points of interest that they need. By keeping track of these points and by searching for others, you'll never find yourself searching for a bathroom again!

Network for a quality experience.

Using Beacon by yourself is fine, but when you start looking and searching for other peoples' beacons, that's when you get the full experience. You can search for keywords and tags to find points of interest in your surroundings.

Built for you!

Beacon was built with the end user in mind, and we need your help to make it work! All you have to do is jump into the app and start laying down those beacons! The more you place, the more you can network with others and make the community grow!



Company Bio

We are a team of college students working on this project like it's our full time job. Any amount would help support and continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated.
